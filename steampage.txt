[b]Better Lights[/b]

[i]Dynamic lights for Garry's Mod.[/i]

[img]https://i.imgur.com/VJ5aKv7.gif[/img]

[hr][/hr]

[b]What It Does[/b]

Better Lights adds extra lighting to weapons, NPCs, items, fires, explosions, projectiles, and more.

[list]
[*]Better flashlight with shadows and optional custom sounds
[*]Muzzle flashes, bullet impacts, and projectile lights
[*]Glowing NPCs, items, Combine eyes, pickups, and effects
[*]Fire, explosion, physgun, toolgun, and gravity gun lights
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[b]Settings[/b]

Open the spawn menu and go to the [b]Better Lights[/b] tab.

You can change colors, brightness, size, decay, flashlight options, and turn each feature on or off.

[b]Recommended[/b]

[b]Use the 64-bit branch of Garry's Mod if you can.[/b]

The addon was made and tested on 64-bit, and the game usually runs better there.

[hr][/hr]

[b]Compatibility[/b]

[list]
[*]Works with GShader Library addons
[*]Works with popular weapon packs like ARC9
[*]Flashlight mods may conflict with the Better Lights flashlight
[*]Other lighting mods may conflict, but features can be disabled separately
[/list]

If you report a flashlight issue, please test without other flashlight addons first.
