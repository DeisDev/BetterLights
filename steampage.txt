[b]Overview[/b]

Better Lights adds dynamic lighting to Garry's Mod, including:

[list]
[*]Flashlight
[*]Muzzle flashes
[*]Bullet impacts
[*]Projectiles
[*]NPC lighting
[*]Item glow
[*]Fires and explosions
[*]Combine eye glow
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[b]Recommended Branch[/b]

[b]It is highly recommended to run this addon on the 64-bit branch.[/b]

This addon was developed and tested on the 64-bit version of Garry's Mod.  
In most cases, the game will run significantly better on this branch.

[hr][/hr]

[b]Configuration[/b]

Open the spawn menu and navigate to the tab:

[b]Better Lights[/b]

Each feature includes configurable options such as:

[list]
[*]Enable / Disable
[*]Color
[*]Size
[*]Brightness
[*]Decay
[*]Flashlight FOV, sway, shadows, and origin offsets
[*]Reset to Defaults
[/list]

The player flashlight module replaces the default flashlight with a projected Better Lights flashlight. It casts shadows, can attach to your weapon, sways and even has it's own custom sounds.

[b]Compatibility & Performance[/b]

[list]
[*]Compatible with GShader Library and its addons
[*]Most effects are client-side with minimal performance impact
[*]Works with popular weapon packs such as ARC9
[*]The player flashlight module will probably conflict with most flashlight mods out there. It can be fully disabled if you wish to use a different flashlight addon. Do not report flashlight related issues without first disabling any other flashlight addons. 
[*]May conflict with other lighting mods, but each module can be disabled individually
[/list]
